Uniform Tickets

Multi-leg slips assembled from strict_mode picks where every leg lives in the same hit-rate band (Elite / Premium / Strong / Value). Backtest tab shows realized joint hit rates and EV across all graded dates.

Rolling per-(size, bucket) joint hit rate and EV on $1 stakes, computed from all graded slates the assembler has touched. Wilson-low is a 95% confidence floor.
